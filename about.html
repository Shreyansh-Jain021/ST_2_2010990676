About Us

We are the largest University

Office of Students Affairs weaves the relationship between the intellectual and social life of the students for their growth and development as matured and responsible human beings. It Organises various curricular and extracurricular activities across all around the year and students also represent University in different Zonal, State and National Level Events.

Office of Students Affairs promotes mutual contact, a democratic outlook and a spirit of oneness among the students across different schools on Campus. It stresses upon the social, cultural and intellectual development of the students.

Office of Students Affairs is an apt platform for the youth talent and tests their creativity in the field of arts, culture and music as well as off-stage events by organizing various competitions. OSA ensures the generation of awareness & consciousness among the students of the events taking place around them with a view to better equipping them as responsible and educated citizens and to build up a healthy students movement.
